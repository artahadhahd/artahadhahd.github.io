FooBar Island Ecosystem Preservation Program in Baz

The FBIEPP is looking for volunteer philanthropists who could help prevent the death of endangered sea creatures and help the locals with famine by providing resources. As there is more and more plastic in the FooBar island, more fish are contaminated with toxic plastic waste. Sea creatures have also started dying rapidly, and it's vital to take action as quickly as possible.

What can YOU do?

Our program mainly needs volunteers to:

  • Remove plastic from the oceans
  • Go hunting or fishing with the locals
  • Extract edibles from inedibles
  • Educate others
  • Give us money

Every single donation counts!

The financial prerequisites of our operation are:

  • $200/day for boat rental
  • $5 education
  • $30 health care per person
  • $30 for a fishing rod
  • $20,000: total cost of transportation

Please support this incredibly difficult and exhausting operation by sending this website to your friends, family and even strangers.

Every single time you log into this website, we make a minor amount of profit out of advertisements, and major search engines like Google are more likely to help us grow.

Many innocent animals and children in poverty would be helped. As of now, about 200 children and 430 adults are starving to death because there's not enough food on the Baz island, and everyday more than 100 fish cease to exist.

And keep in mind: this is totally not a scam.

Our missions:

  • Provide food for the locals
  • Extract plastic from contaminated fish
  • Remove garbage from the island
